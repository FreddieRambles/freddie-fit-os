Wrist traffic light

Green: 0-2/10 and normal next morning.
Yellow: 3-4/10 or lingering ache - reduce load or switch exercises.
Red: sharp thumb-side pain, swelling, clicking with pain, grip weakness, numbness - stop.

Progression order

  2. Reps

  4. Add a set
  5. Increase resistance
  6. Harder variation
  7. Pause or slower lowering
