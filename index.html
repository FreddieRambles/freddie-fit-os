Rest rule

Rest long enough to preserve clean reps. Strength work comes before rushing.

Wrist traffic light

Green: 0–2/10 and normal next morning.
Yellow: 3–4/10 or lingering ache—reduce load, range or volume.
Red: sharp thumb-side pain, swelling, painful clicking, numbness or grip weakness—stop.

Missed-workout protocol

Miss one: shift the schedule forward. Miss two: complete A and B only. Miss a whole week: resume with the previous week's loads. Never double up to punish yourself.

Progression order

  1. Improve technique
  2. Add reps
  3. Reach top of range
  4. Add a set
  5. Increase resistance
  6. Add pause or slower lowering
  7. Use harder variation

Nutrition execution

Start around 2,700–3,000 calories and 140–165 g protein daily. Use four protein feedings and a mass shake when needed. If your seven-day average is flat for two weeks, add 150–200 calories.
